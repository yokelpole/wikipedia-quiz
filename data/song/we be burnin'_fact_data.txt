"We Be Burnin'" is the first single from Sean Paul's third studio album The Trinity. It achieved success worldwide, becoming a top-ten hit on at least ten national music charts, including those of Germany, Italy, the United Kingdom and the United States.


== Background and release ==
"We Be Burnin'" was released as the first single from The Trinity (album) in August 2005 in the US and in September 2005 in the UK. In the U.S., the single peaked inside the Billboard Hot 100 at number six, and in the UK the single was an even bigger hit, peaking at number two, held off number one by The Pussycat Dolls' single "Don't Cha".
