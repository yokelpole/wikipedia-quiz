Tommy Roland Shaw (born September 11, 1953) is an American guitarist, singer, songwriter, and performer best known as the front man and guitarist for the rock band Styx. In between his stints with Styx, he has played with other groups including Damn Yankees, Shaw Blades as well as releasing several solo albums.


== Early life and music career ==
Tommy Shaw was born in Montgomery, Alabama, United States, and played with many local bands in his early years.
