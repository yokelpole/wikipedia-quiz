Richard Lester Liebman (born January 19, 1932), commonly referred to as Dick Lester, is a retired American film director based in the United Kingdom. He is known for his work with the Beatles in the 1960s and his work on the Superman film series in the 1980s.Lester is an Honorary Associate of London Film School.


== Biography ==


=== Early years ===
Richard Lester Liebman was born to a Jewish family in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
