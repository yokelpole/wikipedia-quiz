Clint Edward Lowery (born December 15, 1971) is an American musician, songwriter and producer, best known as the lead guitarist and backing vocalist of the heavy metal band Sevendust. He has also played in Dark New Day and Still Rain and served as the touring guitarist for Korn through most of 2007. In 2008 he decided to work on new music as a solo artist and the name of the project was titled Hello Demons Meet Skeletons.
