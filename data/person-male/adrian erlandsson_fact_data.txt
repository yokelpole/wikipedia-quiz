Adrian Erlandsson (born 27 October 1970 in Malmö, Sweden) is a Swedish heavy metal drummer, who currently plays in At the Gates (1990–1996, 2007–2008, 2010–),  The Haunted (1996–1999, 2013–), Netherbird, and Nemhain. He has also been a member of H.E.A.L. (1994–1996), Hyperhug (1994–1996), Decameron (1997), Cradle of Filth (1999–2006) and Needleye (2006–?), Brujeria (2006–2013) and Yorkshire doom metal band Paradise Lost (2009–2016).
Erlandsson currently resides in London where he owns a photo studio.
