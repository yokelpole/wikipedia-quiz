Benjamin Griffey (born 25 September 1982), better known by his stage name Casper, is a German American rapper signed to the German music label Sony Music.


== Biography ==
Casper was born in Lemgo in North Rhine-Westphalia to a German mother and an American father, Arlen Griffey, who was a soldier stationed in Lemgo. His family moved to Augusta, Georgia, in the United States, when he was two weeks old, where he lived in a trailer park.
