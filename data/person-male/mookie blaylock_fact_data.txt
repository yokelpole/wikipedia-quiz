Daron Oshay "Mookie" Blaylock (born March 20, 1967) is an American retired professional basketball player. He spent 13 years in the National Basketball Association (NBA) with the New Jersey Nets, Atlanta Hawks, and the Golden State Warriors.


== College career ==
A 6 ft 0 in (1.83 m) push-and-pass point guard, he was rated among the best defensive stoppers in the game.
