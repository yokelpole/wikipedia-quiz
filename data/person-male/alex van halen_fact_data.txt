Alexander Arthur van Halen (Dutch: [ɑlɛkˈsɑndər ˈɑrtʏr vɑnˈhaːlə(n)]; born May 8, 1953 in Amsterdam) is an American musician who is the drummer and co-founder of the hard rock band Van Halen. The band was formed in 1974 by Alex Van Halen; his younger brother, Eddie Van Halen; David Lee Roth; and Michael Anthony. Warner Brothers signed the band in 1977, and its debut album was released in 1978.
