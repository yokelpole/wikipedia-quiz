Per Møller Jensen (born 19 November 1973) is a Danish drummer, best known as the former drummer for the Swedish thrash metal band The Haunted. He has earlier played drums in Invocator, Konkhra and Nightrage. He also played drums for Artillery’s Album B.A.C.K.
In 2017, He formed his current band, "The Road"  with guitarist Michael Skovbakke, (Konkhra), bassist Andreas Holma, (Hypocrisy, Scar Symmetry) and vocalist Josh Tyree.
