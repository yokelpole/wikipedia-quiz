Shawn Colvin (born Shawna Lee Colvin, January 10, 1956) is an American singer-songwriter and musician. While Colvin has been a solo recording artist for decades, she is perhaps best known for her 1997 Grammy Award-winning song "Sunny Came Home".


== Early life ==
Colvin was born Shawna Lee Colvin in Vermillion, South Dakota, and spent her youth in  Carbondale, Illinois and London, Ontario, Canada.
