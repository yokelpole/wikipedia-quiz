Broadcast Music, Inc. (BMI) is one of the four major United States performing rights organizations, along with ASCAP, SESAC, and GMR. It collects license fees from businesses that use music on behalf of songwriters, composers, and music publishers and distributes them as royalties to those members whose works have been performed.  In FY 2019, BMI collected $1.28 billion in revenues and distributed $1.196 billion in royalties.
