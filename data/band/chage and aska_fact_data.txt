Chage and Aska (チャゲ&飛鳥) are a Japanese popular music duo composed of male singer-songwriters from Fukuoka Prefecture: Chage (チャゲ) (born January 6, 1958) and Aska (飛鳥涼（RYO Aska)) (born February 24, 1958). To date they have sold over 31 million albums and singles in Japan.


== History ==
They were formed in the late 1970s at the suggestion of the A&R division of the Yamaha Music Foundation.
