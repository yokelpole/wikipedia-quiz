Inga DeCarlo Fung Marchand (born September 6, 1978), of the stage name Foxy Brown, is an American rapper who emerged into stardom surrounding her debut album Ill Na Na, released in 1996. She was also part of the short-lived rap group the Firm.
Her second album Chyna Doll, arrived in 1999, and her third, the Grammy-nominated Broken Silence, in 2001.
