Roskilde (UK:  ROSK-il-ə, also US:  RUSK-ee-lə, Danish: [ˈʁɒskilə]), located 30 km (19 mi) west of Copenhagen on the Danish island of Zealand, is the main city in the eponymous municipality. With a population of 51,121 (as of 1 January 2019), the city is a business and educational centre for the region and the 10th largest city in Denmark. It is governed by the administrative council of Roskilde Municipality.
