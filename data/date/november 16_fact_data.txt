November 16 is the 320th day of the year (321st in leap years) in the Gregorian calendar.  45 days remain until the end of the year.


== Events ==
534 – Justinian I, who was an Eastern Roman (Byzantine) emperor in Constantinople, approves and publishes the second and final revision of the Codex Justinianus
951 – Emperor Li Jing sends a Southern Tang expeditionary force of 10,000 men under Bian Hao to conquer Chu.
