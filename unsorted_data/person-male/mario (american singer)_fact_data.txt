Mario Dewar Barrett (born August 27, 1986), known mononymously as Mario, is an American entertainer, singer, songwriter, actor, and model. He is known for his singles "Just a Friend" (2002) and "Let Me Love You" (2004), which won him two Billboard Music Awards. He has released four studio albums: Mario (2002), Turning Point (2004), Go (2007), and DNA (2009).
