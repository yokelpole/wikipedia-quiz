The fourteenth season of the American reality The Voice premiered on February 26, 2018, on NBC with Blake Shelton and Adam Levine returning as coaches once again, making it their fourteenth season on the show. Returning coach Alicia Keys returned to the panel for her third season and New coach Kelly Clarkson joined the coaching panel.On May 22, 2018, Brynn Cartelli was crowned the winner of The Voice. With her win, the fifteen-year-old became the youngest winner in the show's history.
